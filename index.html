About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineering student at Tufts University. My current work involves projects that aim to integrate LEGO, and Arduino into engineering education. I am passionate about robotics and automation and how coupled with 3D printing, they can be used to improve the manufacturing process of a variety of products.

I am also interested in the development of new materials for industrial manufacturing, and how they can be used to improve the quality of products.

Research

This past summer, I worked at the Tufts Center for Engineering Education and Outreach (CEEO) where I helped prepare STEM and Engineering education materials for middle school and high school kids. Our work also involved finding ways of helping teachers in underfunded schools in rural Rwanda incorporate hand-on activities in their engineering curriculum. We were able to work with over 20 schools in Rwanda to establish makerspaces and trained over 42 teachers.
